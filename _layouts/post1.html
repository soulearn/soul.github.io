Unrevealed bridge

Article by Anonymous

There was a lonely lad sitting near the waste To the real world he was just part of the sight But a real meal for dogs My thoughts chose to loiter through his mind Forlornness and solitude seemed like companion of his eyes Mixture of curiosity and concern rolled my body towards him Skin was desiccated and tears had made visible path down his chin Hesitantly I sat beside him But he din bother and kept staring at the blue sky People passed by staring at my one sided conversation And surprisingly he turned his face towards me No smile, no expression , he stared at me And he kept staring at me Finally he burst down asking me a question WILL YOU BE MY WINGS TO FLY And the words echoed WILL YOU?? WILL YOU BE MY WINGS?
