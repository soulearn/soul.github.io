My Journey in SoUL

The Walk under moonlight

Article by Deepak Kumar

Joining SoUL has been one of the best decisions I have made during my engineering days. I will cherish the memories I made by being part of the organization. I learned to teach, to be creative, to plan, the importance of teamwork, patience and most of all to be responsible. Apart from teaching kids, I was also part of various activities taken up by the organization such as cloth drive, book drive, raising funds to help people during Chennai floods to name a few. It was difficult for me to initiate a conversation with people but by being part of these activities, I was able to overcome that difficulty. I was also part of the team teaching 10th std boys. Both of them cleared their board exams in the first attempt. It was a huge accomplishment for the team as well as the kids. I miss those innocent smiles. Amongst them, I was a kid again. Lastly, our seniors were a great inspiration for our batch. I wish all the very best for the present batch of SoUL.

-Zee
